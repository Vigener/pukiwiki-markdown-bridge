# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how "PukiWiki Markdown Bridge" (the "Extension") handles your data.

## Data Collection and Usage
The Extension does **NOT** collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information, browsing history, or website content to any external servers. 

- **Website Content:** The Extension accesses the content of the PukiWiki edit text area solely for the purpose of converting it between PukiWiki syntax and Markdown syntax locally within your browser. 
- **Storage:** The Extension uses Chrome's local storage sync (`chrome.storage.sync`) solely to save your custom URL preferences. This data remains within your Google account's synchronized storage and is never transmitted to the developer or third parties.

## Third-Party Services
The Extension does not use any third-party analytics or tracking services.

## Changes to this Policy
If any changes are made to this privacy policy, they will be updated in this repository.

## Contact
For any questions or concerns, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.
